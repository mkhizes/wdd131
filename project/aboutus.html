About Us

Our mission is to bridge the gap between businesses
and communities, reducing poverty and increasing trust.
The chamber of commerce definition is a local organization
that gathers business leaders working in the community.
The intention is to advance the goals and interests of the
organization and others operating in the area. It's
usually a group of locally owned businesses that have a
common interest.The Chamber of Commerce Network exists to
support and connect companies, bringing together firms to
build new relationships, share best practice and foster
new opportunities.
