Site Purpose

To assist,uplift and unite Businesses while doing the same for the community

Because of high unemployment rate in my country, particularly in my city Durban where most people flock in numbers to look for work. I chose this subject with the intention of uniting the business sector, and uplifting upcoming small businesses so that they can be in touch with the communities that are benefiting from their services hence this will increase trust and lessen crime as others will be hired when job opportunities increase. These businesses can also sponsor skills learning programs such as bricklaying, tiling, electrical installation, plumbing, etc. In this way, we will curb poverty and crime at the same time. Even awareness campaigns will be supported by these businesses.

Audience

My audience is the Small Micro Medium Enterprises, even Commercial business people who operating under Ethekwini Metropolitan Municipality and surrounding. I am targeting the community as this organisation has a database of their service providers, that they may be aware of what these businesses do for the community other than offering them services.
